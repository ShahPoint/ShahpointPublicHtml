ABOUT

We are a passionate team of SharePoint developers and engineers based out of the bustling city of Pittsburgh.

Our practice grew out demand for our focused skills related to Microsoft’s integrated communications platforms, cloud-based architectures, and big-data management. Headquartered in Pittsburgh, we are strategically located within reach of major Eastern and Midwest businesses centers and have access to a rich pool of commercial and academic technology experts.

ShahPoint is recognized for delivering effective, yet simple solutions. We deliver timely solutions at a fraction of the time or cost of many industry integrators.

Simplicity

Our agile development philosophy creates code that’s faster and easier to extend into the future. We are intensely mindful of the human experience too. IT transitions are often accompanied by user stress as employees learn new systems. It’s our experience that simple solutions are more likely to be adopted and supported by your end users.

Uptime

It takes a strong team to stand behind mission critical software in the cloud. We’re experienced with demanding benchmarks for businesses that rely on cloud-based data and communications. Our design methodology is guided by the necessity for Stability and Uptime.

Scalable

Straight forward solutions provide higher long term value as they’re designed to scale more easily and at a more fixed cost. Our second design goal is to provide a clear and extensible code base that can keep up with your ever changing business needs.

OUR SERVICES

Solving your business problems


Sharepoint Server Solutions

We understand some business units still prefer to work within private corporate networks (e.g., products in sensitive Financial and HIPPA industries). Premise-based SharePoint solutions still ensure all the standardization and power of the leading platform. ShahPoint understands your need for flexibility and control and can help design and integrate around your existing secure private infrastructure.

Sharepoint Migrations

Our core practice is built around SharePoint migration. Are you considering a transition from another platform such as Lotus Notes? Are you like many clients who implemented Exchange some years back but never committed to the full communications suite? We have you covered! We can help solve your issues and move you to a single platform for messaging, calendars, notes, shared-data, and enterprise-wide management controls. Let us help make your upgrades and transition as smooth as possible.

Custom Solutions and Software Development

ShahPoint Services develops custom software for clients. Is your next business opportunity dependent on delivering cross-platform mobile apps, managing the explosion of data from connected-devices, leveraging real-time communications, capturing a new factory automation process, or improving human-computer interaction (HCI). Let our team meet with you to understand and recommend solutions for your particular situation and needs.

Azure Solutions

Leverage Microsoft’s powerful Cloud based solutions using industry leading hosting technology. As a cloud environment, Azure provides the scaffolding to more easily connect with powerful web API’s and cutting edge data tools. Azure relieves the headaches of maintaining web servers, defrays the costs of software ownership, and ensures that users will always be able to get to your site.

Email Migrations

Hosted email is a ‘killer app’ that can provide immediate ROI to your business. Why struggle with email servers or risk downtime? Let us help you move to the industry’s most trusted business solution, Microsoft Exchange.

Solution Training

A solution is only as effective as the data it captures. Often, the critical input stage begins with trained personnel. We back every deliverable with the option for extended, or onsite training so you can get the most out of your new software.
